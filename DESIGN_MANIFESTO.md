# Design Manifesto

Nothing exists only because it looks cool.

Every object must tell part of the story.

Darkness is a design element.

Silence is a design element.

Negative space is a design element.

Restraint is more valuable than complexity.

Do not explain personality.
Reveal it.

Do not imitate motorsport.
Capture its philosophy.

Do not imitate engineering.
Practice it.

Technology exists to support emotion.

If something feels like AI generated, remove it.

If something could exist on another portfolio unchanged, redesign it.

The goal is not visual impressiveness.

The goal is quiet memorability.
